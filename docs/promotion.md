## Midiano Wording

Use this wording:

```text
The demo videos were recorded from MIDI playback in Midiano.
```

Avoid this wording:

```text
Generated with Midiano.
```

Reason: Midiano is used here as a playback and visualization tool. The MIDI files are generated by the bundled Python script and the Codex skill workflow.

## FAQ Risks

### Is this generated by Midiano?

No. Midiano is used to play back and visualize the generated MIDI files in the demo videos.

### Is this finished production music?

No. v0.1 is a MIDI-first prototyping workflow. For production use, load the MIDI into a DAW, choose better instruments or SoundFonts, adjust arrangement and mix, then render audio.

### Is this only field music?

No. v0.1 started with field/exploration examples and now includes early active battle and final boss battle tests. The strongest public examples are still the field tracks, while battle and boss coverage is being expanded.

### What exactly does the skill output?

The most concrete output is Standard MIDI (`.mid`). The skill can also produce composition briefs, loop plans, MIDI-like JSON, stem plans, and engine handoff notes.

### Why not generate WAV directly?

MIDI is editable, inspectable, small, and easy to route into different sound sources. WAV/OGG rendering is planned once the SoundFont or DAW rendering path is stronger.
